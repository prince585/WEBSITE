Mint – Grow Guide

🌱 About Mint

Mint is a cooling herb used in teas, chutneys, and medicines. It’s very easy to grow at home.

🛠️ What You Need

  • A small pot or bottle
  • Soil (moist, well-drained)
  • Mint stem (cut from existing plant)
  • Sunlight (partial)

🪴 How to Grow

  1. Take a fresh mint stem and remove bottom leaves
  2. Place it in water until roots grow (5–7 days)
  3. Plant it in soil and water lightly
  4. Keep in a semi-sunny spot
